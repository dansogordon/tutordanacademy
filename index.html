Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your free business English session and ongoing lessons.

What will I do in the free 20-minute session?

We’ll have a relaxed, 1:1 conversation focused on topics that matter to you — business, hobbies, travel, daily life, etc. I’ll evaluate your level and give personalized tips to help you improve quickly.

Will these lessons help me in my career?

Yes! By practicing business English in realistic conversations, you’ll gain confidence, improve communication, and stand out at work — making promotions, raises, and international opportunities more accessible.

Do I need to prepare anything for the free session?

No preparation is required! Just bring your questions or topics you want to practice. I’ll guide the conversation and make it productive, fun, and focused on your goals.

Can I choose the topics for our lessons?

Absolutely. While free sessions are open talking, ongoing lessons can be fully tailored to your professional needs — presentations, meetings, emails, negotiations, or anything you want to focus on.

How often should I take lessons to see real progress?

Most students see noticeable improvement with 2–3 sessions per week. You can start with the frequency that fits your schedule and increase as you get comfortable.

What if I want a video class versus an audio-only class?

You can choose either format for your free session. Video is great for visual cues, pronunciation, and presentations. Audio is flexible for commuting or busy schedules.
